How It Started?

A home based business runned by myself, an 18 year old student, with the exceptional help of my mother. I was inspired to start Hadaf in November 2018 as a distraction from my diagnosis of type 1 diabetes.

We initially planned on specializing in scarves, mainly chiffon scarves. However, we were fortunate enough to be able to expand and add clothing and bags to our collection, Alhamdulliah! We try our utmost best to keep our fashion up to date in order to deliver what YOU, the customers, are interested in Insha-Allah!
